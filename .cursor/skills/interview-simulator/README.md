# Interview Simulator

An AI skill that simulates real coding interview dynamics — asking follow-up questions, probing edge cases, and challenging you to optimize, just like a real interviewer would.

## What It Does

- Asks **follow-up questions** after you solve a problem (complexity, edge cases, optimization, extensions)
- Simulates different **interviewer styles** (Socratic, edge-case-focused, optimization-focused, system-design-oriented)
- Provides **structured feedback** on your interview performance
- Tracks your **improvement over time** via MEMORY.md

## How to Use

- *"Interview me on two-sum"* — starts a full interview simulation
- *"Give me follow-up questions"* — after solving, get interviewer-style probes
- *"How would I do in an interview?"* — get a performance assessment
- *"Practice explaining my approach"* — focus on communication skills

## How It Works

1. Lets you solve the problem (with coaching from leetcode-coach if needed)
2. Runs through progressively harder follow-up rounds
3. Evaluates your responses on 6 dimensions (understanding, approach, communication, correctness, complexity, optimization)
4. Records patterns in your interview performance for future sessions
